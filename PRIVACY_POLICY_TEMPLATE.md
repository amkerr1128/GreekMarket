## Who we are
- GreekMarket is a marketplace for college-community users to create accounts, list items, message other users, and complete purchases of physical goods.

## What information we collect
- Account information you provide, such as name, email address, handle, school, and chapter affiliation
- Content you upload, such as profile photos, chapter images, listing photos, listing text, and messages
- Transaction-related information needed to complete physical-goods purchases
- Technical information needed to run and secure the service, such as log data, device/browser data, and IP-related security signals

## How we use your information
- To create and maintain your account
- To display your profile and listings
- To connect buyers and sellers
- To process payouts and purchases through payment providers
- To store and deliver uploaded media
- To support moderation, safety, fraud prevention, and legal compliance
- To improve product quality and reliability

## Third-party services
- Stripe for payment processing and seller payouts
- Cloudinary for image/media hosting
- Your hosting provider and infrastructure vendors for storage, logs, uptime, and security

## Physical goods purchases
- GreekMarket is intended for transactions involving physical goods and related local-marketplace activity.
- Payment processing may be handled by a third-party processor.
